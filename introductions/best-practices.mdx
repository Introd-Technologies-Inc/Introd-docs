---
title: "Best Practices"
description: "Use connector time well and keep warm introduction workflows high quality."
---

Warm intros work best when the request is specific, earned, and easy for the connector to evaluate.

## Recommended habits

- prefer the strongest credible connector over the closest weak one
- explain why the target matters now
- keep asks concise and context rich
- respect stale or uncertain evidence
- use outcomes to improve the next request

## What to avoid

- spamming every possible connector
- treating a low-confidence path as production-ready
- hiding uncertainty from the requester or connector
