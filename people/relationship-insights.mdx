title: "Relationship Insights"
description: "Use trust, freshness, and context to decide how to act on a target relationship."
---

Relationship insight is the difference between a list of names and a decision system.

![Contact profile and path insights in Introd](/images/product/contact-profile.png)

## Core signals

- TrustRank and graph confidence
- recency of contact or communication evidence
- connector strength and role fit
- company and community context around the target

## What a user should learn from this view

A strong relationship insight view should answer:

- is this route actionable now
- who should make the ask
- what evidence supports that decision
- what is missing or getting stale

## What users should leave with

A clear answer to whether the relationship is actionable now, who should move on it, and what evidence still needs to be gathered.

## Use insight before action

Open relationship insight before sending a high-stakes request. Confirm the relationship is fresh, the connector is appropriate, and the context does not make the request awkward or low quality.
