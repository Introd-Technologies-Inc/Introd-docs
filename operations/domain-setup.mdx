## Target state

- primary docs domain: `https://docs.getintrod.ai`
- canonical URL: `https://docs.getintrod.ai`
- sitemap: `https://docs.getintrod.ai/sitemap.xml`
- robots: `https://docs.getintrod.ai/robots.txt`

## DNS and TLS sequence

1. Add `docs.getintrod.ai` as a Mintlify custom domain.
2. Publish the verification TXT records Mintlify provides.
3. Wait until verification is complete and TLS is ready.
4. Point the `docs` CNAME for `getintrod.ai` at Mintlify.
5. Re-test canonical pages, sitemap, and robots.

Use [Cloudflare Cutover](/operations/cloudflare-cutover) when the `getintrod.ai` zone is managed in Cloudflare.

## Keep FoundersNest online

Do not repoint `docs.foundersnest.io` as part of the Introd cutover. Treat it as a separate legacy docs domain until the Introd launch is stable.
