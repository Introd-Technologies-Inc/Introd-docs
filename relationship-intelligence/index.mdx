## Core concepts

| Concept | What it answers |
| --- | --- |
| Warm introductions | Is there a credible route into this person or company? |
| Connector ranking | Who is the best person to make the intro? |
| TrustRank | How much confidence should the system place in the route? |
| Relationship strength | How fresh, direct, and proven is the connector's edge? |
| Evidence chips | What concrete signals support the recommendation? |
| Graph confidence | How certain is the system that the route is worth action? |

## Non-negotiables

- Do not fabricate a warm path.
- Do not overstate stale evidence.
- Do not hide missing data behind confident UI.
- Do explain why a connector is strong, weak, or unavailable.
