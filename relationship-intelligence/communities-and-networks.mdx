Introd should be able to reason across more than a flat contact list. Communities, alumni clusters, and investor networks provide additional structure for finding credible paths.

![Alumni and VC communities in Introd](/images/product/alumni-network.png)

![Groups in Introd](/images/product/groups.png)

## Community layers

| Layer | Example use |
| --- | --- |
| Communities | Find trusted connectors in curated groups |
| Alumni networks | Re-rank paths based on school, cohort, or operator overlap |
| Investor networks | Find the connectors most likely to unlock investor access |

## Design rule

These network layers should improve ranking and explainability. They should not be treated as proof by themselves. A shared school or community is context, not trust.
