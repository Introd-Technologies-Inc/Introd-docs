Alumni Networks help Introd understand communities that matter for access, trust, and warm introductions.

![Alumni and VC communities in Introd](/images/product/alumni-network.png)

## What you can join

- university alumni communities
- company alumni communities
- VC portfolio communities
- investor and operator networks

## How alumni context helps

Alumni and portfolio communities can make a path more explainable, especially when the connector and target share a meaningful network.

## Important boundary

A shared community is useful context, not proof of trust by itself. Introd should still rely on connector quality, freshness, and route evidence before recommending an intro.
