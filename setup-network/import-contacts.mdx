description: "Bring existing contacts into Introd from CSV, Gmail, Outlook, or other email sources."
---

Import Contacts helps you fill gaps in your network when integrations alone do not cover everyone you know.

![Import contacts in Introd](/images/product/import-contacts.png)

## Import options

- CSV file
- Gmail
- Outlook
- other email providers

## CSV imports

Use CSV import when you already have a clean contact list. Include names, emails, companies, and job titles when available.

## Review before importing

Before you import, confirm that:

- the preview looks correct
- duplicate rows are expected or cleaned up
- important fields such as name and email are present
- you are importing contacts you are allowed to store in Introd

## After importing

Imported contacts should become available for search, collections, contacts, and path discovery once Introd has processed them.
