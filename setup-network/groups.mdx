Groups help teams and communities coordinate around shared networks.

![Groups in Introd](/images/product/groups.png)

## What groups help with

- tracking active communities
- finding members who can help
- surfacing unread updates and active discussions
- coordinating around shared relationship goals

## Group insights

Group insights show engagement, member growth, and suggested groups worth joining.

## Good group hygiene

Keep groups focused. A group should represent a real community or workflow, not a catch-all bucket.
