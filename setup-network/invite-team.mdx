Invite Team lets a workspace combine relationship context across trusted members. Team access should improve coverage without making relationship data feel uncontrolled.

![Team management in Introd](/images/product/team.png)

## What team setup controls

- who belongs to the workspace
- which members can request introductions
- whether intro approval is required
- which members have admin access
- whether pending invites still need follow-up

## Roles

| Role | Typical access |
| --- | --- |
| Admin | Manage members, settings, and intro approval rules. |
| Member | Contribute relationship context and request introductions according to policy. |
| View only | Inspect approved workspace context without making changes. |

## Invite workflow

1. Open Setup Network.
2. Select **Invite Team**.
3. Copy an invite link or invite members directly.
4. Confirm default access and intro approval rules.
5. Review pending invites until they are accepted or revoked.

## Approval rules

Use stricter approval when teammates can request intros through relationships they do not personally own. Use lighter approval only when the team has a clear trust policy and strong operating norms.

## Good team hygiene

- remove inactive members
- keep admins limited
- review pending invites
- document who approves intros
- audit workspace settings after team changes
