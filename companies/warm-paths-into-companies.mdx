---
title: "Warm Paths Into Companies"
description: "Resolve an account-level opportunity into specific people, routes, and next actions."
---

Warm paths into companies should collapse account strategy into concrete human routes.

## Flow

1. choose the company
2. identify the target roles or people
3. rank the strongest connector for each route
4. move the best path into an introduction workflow

## UX expectation

Users should see which route is strongest, why it wins, and which fallback path comes next if the first connector is unavailable.
