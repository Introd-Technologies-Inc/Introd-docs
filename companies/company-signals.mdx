title: "Understanding Company Signals"
description: "Use account-level signals to interpret whether a company is worth pursuing through the network."
---

Company signals help users separate a promising target from a merely interesting one.

![Company signals in Introd](/images/product/company-search.png)

## Signals that matter

- connector density across the company
- reachable seniority or function coverage
- recency of evidence tied to the account
- community overlap such as alumni or investor networks

## How to use them

Signals should sharpen prioritization. They should not replace the human-level explanation of who will make the intro and why that route is credible.

## Signal quality

Treat a signal as stronger when it is recent, supported by multiple sources, and tied to a specific person or team. Treat it as weaker when it only describes the company broadly.
