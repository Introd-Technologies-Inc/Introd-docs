Company intelligence turns a target account into a reachable opportunity map.

![Company search and intelligence in Introd](/images/product/company-search.png)

## What this view should answer

- why this company matters right now
- which roles are worth targeting first
- whether the network has real access or only thin coverage
- what evidence makes the account reachable

## Output quality bar

The company view should read like account strategy, not a scraped directory dump.
