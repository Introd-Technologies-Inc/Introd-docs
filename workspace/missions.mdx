Missions connect your goals to the people, companies, and warm paths that can help you reach them.

![Missions in Introd](/images/product/missions.png)

## What missions are for

Use a mission when you have a clear outcome, such as:

- raising a seed round
- hiring a founding engineer
- landing enterprise customers
- building an advisor board
- expanding into a new market

## What to track

Each mission should show:

- current progress
- warm path score
- introductions created
- next best action
- the people most likely to unlock the goal

## How to use missions well

Keep missions specific. A mission should guide action, not become a vague folder of contacts.
