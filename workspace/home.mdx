Home is the daily starting point for Introd. It shows what is happening in your network and helps you decide what to do next without opening every section manually.

![Introd home dashboard](/images/product/home.png)

## What you can do from Home

- search for people, companies, investors, and other targets
- review recommended introductions
- continue active introduction requests
- open saved lists
- check network health and setup progress

## Recommended introductions

Recommended introductions highlight targets where Introd has enough evidence to suggest a useful path. Review the connector and confidence before sending a request.

## Active introductions

Active introductions show requests already in motion. Use this list to spot requests that are pending, in progress, completed, or waiting for follow-up.

## Network health

Network health summarizes whether your data sources are fresh enough to support good recommendations. If the score is low, go back to setup and improve coverage before relying on ranking.
