Home is the daily starting point for Introd. It combines search, recommended intro paths, recent activity, communities, and network health so you can decide what to do next without opening every section manually.

![Introd home dashboard](/images/product/home.png)

## What Home should answer

- Which warm paths are ready to review?
- Which requests or replies need attention?
- Is the network healthy enough to trust recommendations?
- Which communities are adding useful reach?
- What changed since the last sync?

## Recommended intro paths

Recommended paths show targets that are reachable through credible connectors. Do not treat a recommendation as permission to send immediately. Open the path, review the evidence, then decide whether to request an intro.

Each row should make these fields visible:

| Field | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Target | The person or company you want to reach. |
| Connector | The person who can credibly make the handoff. |
| Path strength | Whether the route is strong enough to act on. |
| Likelihood | A summary of success probability and quality. |
| Action | The next step, usually path review or intro request. |

## Network health

Network health summarizes whether your sources are fresh enough to support recommendations. If the score is low, go back to setup before relying on ranking.

Watch for:

- stale sync timestamps
- low relationship coverage
- weak relationship diversity
- declining response rate
- missing LinkedIn, Google, or Microsoft sources

## Recent activity

Recent activity is where Home becomes operational. Use it to catch accepted requests, replies, profile views, and newly discovered connections before they become stale.

## Communities

Community cards show where network reach is coming from. If a community is active and aligned with your goals, use it to discover new paths or strengthen weak ones.
