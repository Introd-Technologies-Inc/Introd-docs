Settings is where account, notification, privacy, integration, and billing controls live. Treat it as the control plane for how Introd uses your data and how your workspace receives updates.

![Settings in Introd](/images/product/settings.png)

## Account

Use Account settings to update profile identity, email preferences, password settings, two-factor authentication, active sessions, and data visibility.

## Privacy and security

Privacy settings control how your profile and graph context are visible. Before expanding team access, confirm:

- profile visibility
- search visibility
- data personalization settings
- export and deletion options
- active sessions
- two-factor authentication state

## Integrations

The Integrations panel shows connected and available sources such as Google Contacts, Microsoft 365, LinkedIn, and Salesforce. Use it to reconnect sources, review status, and find providers that are not yet contributing signal.

## Notifications

Keep introduction and request notifications enabled if Introd is part of an active workflow. Disable marketing emails if you only want operational updates.

## Danger zone

Account deactivation and deletion are permanent controls. Before using them, export required data and confirm whether workspace-owned data should remain available to teammates.
