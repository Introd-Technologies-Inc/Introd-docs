Saved lists help you turn search into an organized workflow. Use them when you want to track a group of people or companies over time.

![Saved lists in Introd](/images/product/saved-lists.png)

## Common list types

- investors for a fundraise
- product leaders at target accounts
- alumni network contacts
- strategic partners
- board candidates and advisors

## When to create a list

Create a list when a search result should become part of an ongoing workflow. Lists are useful when you need to compare routes, revisit targets, or share context with a team.

## What a good list contains

- a clear name
- a short purpose
- the people or companies being tracked
- owner and visibility settings
- recent update activity
