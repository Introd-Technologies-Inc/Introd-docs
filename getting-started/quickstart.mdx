Use this page when you want the shortest path from zero setup to a usable warm-intro workflow.

![Introd onboarding flow](/images/product/onboarding.png)

## What you need

- an Introd account
- a LinkedIn account you actively use
- Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 access if you want stronger evidence right away
- one person or company you actually want to reach

## Setup in five steps

1. Create your account and finish sign-in.
   You should land in onboarding, not an empty workspace.

2. Connect LinkedIn first.
   This gives Introd first-degree network coverage and unlocks the extension workflow.

3. Connect Gmail or Outlook.
   Communication evidence helps Introd rank connectors by freshness and trust instead of social adjacency alone.

4. Let Introd build your network.
   Wait for the first sync to finish before judging path quality.

5. Search a real target and inspect the recommended path.
   Open the route, review the connector, and only create a request if the evidence is credible.

## What success looks like

After a healthy first setup, you should be able to:

- search for a person or company that matters now
- see at least one ranked connector or a truthful "not enough evidence" state
- understand why the suggested route is strong or weak
- draft your first introduction request without rewriting all the context by hand

## If you do not see warm paths yet

Usually one of these is true:

- LinkedIn has not finished syncing
- Google or Microsoft evidence has not been connected yet
- the target is outside your current network coverage
- the route exists, but the evidence is too weak or stale to recommend confidently

That is normal early on. Introd should tell the truth about missing evidence instead of pretending every route is ready.
