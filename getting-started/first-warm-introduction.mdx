The first warm introduction flow should prove that Introd can move from data collection to a concrete, explainable action.

![Warm intro path in Introd](/images/product/warm-intro-path.png)

## Prerequisites

- an active account
- at least one completed LinkedIn sync
- enough graph coverage to show one or more candidate connectors
- a search target in people or company search

## End-to-end flow

1. Open [People](/people) or [Companies](/companies).
2. Search for the target you care about.
3. Inspect the proposed warm path and why the route was ranked.
4. Confirm the connector has enough trust and freshness to act.
5. Open the intro compose flow.
6. Save a draft, send immediately, or schedule for later.
7. Track the outcome from the introductions surface.
8. Feed the outcome back into Introd so future connector ranking improves.

## What a good intro draft includes

- a clear target name and company
- the connector selected from real network evidence
- enough context for the connector to decide whether the request is appropriate
- realistic follow-up expectations

## What not to do

- do not force an intro when the graph says confidence is weak
- do not hide stale or decaying relationship evidence
- do not treat shared employer or tag overlap as proof of trust
