---

Use this page as a quick glossary while you learn the product.

## Warm path

A route from you to a target person through one or more real connectors with enough evidence to explain why the path might work.

## Connector

The person Introd recommends as the best person to make the introduction.

## TrustRank

Introd's explanation of how trustworthy and usable a route looks right now based on evidence, freshness, and prior outcomes.

## Graph confidence

How safe it is to act on a suggested route without overclaiming what the system knows.

## Evidence chips

Short proof points such as recent emails, meetings, shared networks, or prior introductions that explain why a path ranks well.

## Relationship strength

How direct, recent, and proven a relationship edge is between two people.

## Community context

Helpful overlap such as alumni, investor, or operator networks. This supports a route, but it is not proof by itself.

## Intro request

The forwardable ask a user sends through a connector once the route is strong enough to act on.
